Places to Visit in Goa

Butterfly island

Discover the best of Goa on a 6-day tour. Visit the beaches of North and South Goa, explore the Butterfly Island, and enjoy a boat ride to the island and adventurous water activities.

Baga beach

Embark on a guided trip to Goa and visit all the city highlights. Admire the jaw-dropping beaches and see The Basilica of Bom Jesus, Aguada Fort, Old Goa, and many more famous sites in one day.

statue of abbe faria

The historic statue made of bronze was installed in the state capital in 1945 and depicts Abbe Faria hypnotising a woman. The statue is located at a small square just near the Secretariat Building in Panjim.
